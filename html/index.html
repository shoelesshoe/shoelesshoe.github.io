Think we still can wait? It's already happening

The impacts of climate change are omnipresent in our everyday lives. Just look around you.

Beach Filled with Trash

No rubbish bins near the beach? Just leave or dump your trash on the sand. No one would know, right? That's how our once clean beaches become polluted with garbage.

Sea Pollution

The garbage then floats into the once clean waters rich with marine life, polluting the waters with plastic bags which can trick marine animals into thinking they are food to consume, ultimately killing them.

Melting Ice

The greenhouse gases that we emit from cars, burning fuel for electricity etc. pollutes the air and heat up the Earth more quickly by trapping heat, as a result raising global temperatures and melting ice and polar caps.
